Amartya 10:35 AM

Based on your symptoms — headache, mild fever, and fatigue — here are the most likely possibilities:

Possible Causes
  • Viral infection (Common Cold/Flu) — Most likely cause. Usually self-limiting.
  • Dengue fever — If fever is high and there's joint pain. Needs attention.
  • Dehydration — Can cause headache and fatigue alongside fever.
  • Stress or exhaustion — Weakens immunity and causes these symptoms.
What You Should Do
  • Rest well and stay hydrated (8–10 glasses of water/day)
  • Take paracetamol (500mg) for fever if above 38°C
  • Avoid cold drinks and oily foods
  • Monitor temperature every 4–6 hours
See a doctor if: Fever exceeds 39°C, symptoms worsen after 48 hours, or you develop rash, breathing difficulty, or severe body ache.
